Hiển thị bộ đệm cho trang web (1)
Em co dang ky mot bo dem cho trang Web ca nhan, tai trang web mien phi 123Counters.com va da dưoc cap mot doan code, khi em chen vao trang web thi van khong thay bo dem xuat hien ( Trang Web em chua upload ), va duoi doan code do co dong: 
"Note: You must post both the hit counter and the sponsored link on the same page or your counter will be disabled, or we will change the counter image to an advertisement."
Em khong hieu nhieu ve dong nay cac anh chi vui long giai thich va huong dan giup cach lam cho bo dem duoc hien thi o trang Web cua em. Tran trong cam on.

Chao ban minhhung! Minh khong biet la ban khong hieu nghia cua dong chu do hay khong hieu tai sao lai co dong chu do. Dieu nay co the la do ban chua upload trang web co doan ma cua bo dem len. Nen duong link cua nguoi bao tro khong the hien duoc. Ban hay thu upload trang web len roi xem ket qua the nao.
