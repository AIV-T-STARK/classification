Ban khong xem phim duoc tu monitor khac boi vi chuong trinh coi phim cua ban khong cho phep ban coi cung 1 luc 2 man hinh. Ban chi co the coi phim tai 1 trong 2 man hinh ma thoi. ban co download driver ve cung nhu the thoi, khong giai quyet duoc van de gi ca. Muon coi phim o monitor khac hay projector thi ban phai bam vao phim "Fn" (function) va phim chuyen man hinh hien thi (thuong thi no co dau hieu la 2 cai man hinh), ban bam 2 lan, la duoc.
