Hỏi cách nối mạng 3 máy tính dùng 1 hub (1)
Lam on chi giup cho em cach noi mang 3 may tinh tro len, dung mot hub:- cach setup phan cung- cach cai dat phan men (may cai win98): tung buoc lam nhu the nao.Em xin chan thanh cam on!
Le Thi Trang
Hồi âm:
De noi mang 3 may vao 1 hub rat don gian. Ban se theo cac buoc sau day:
1.Cap mang (thong dung la cap RJ45). Neu dung cho 3 may thi can den 3 soi day (luu y day khong dai qua 100m) 
2.Connector UTP: 6 cai cho 3 may (dau bam cap).
3.Bam cap theo chuan T586B (hay goi la chuan B).
4.Cam card mang vao moi may va cai driver cho Card mang. Cai xong Boot may lai.
5.Day la phan nghi thuc mang hay con goi la Protocol.
6.Sau khi ban Boot may lai thi tren Desktop co bieu tuong NetworkNieghborhood va bam chuot phai vao bieu tuong nay va chon Properties. Chon lop Identification, chon o workgroup. Ban go vao ten workgroup (ten nay do ban tu dat va bat buoc ten nay 3 may phai giong nhau.nghia la co cung ten workgroup).
7.Chon vao lop configuration de Add them TCP/IP va NetBui (neu 1 trong 2 chua co trong lop nay).Chuc ban thanh cong.
