1. Ban nen check lai xem tat ca cac cable da cam chat giua main va cac bo phan chua. Ngoai ra thi check quat gan vao main, check ram va tat ca cac bo phan khac. Neu chay pentium 4 HT thi nen dung nguon 400W se on dinh.
